registration

Creating a new account on Sciteit is just about as quick and painless as can be.

You don't need to tell us your name.

You don't need to click on a link in your email.

In fact, you don't even need to tell us your email address (though if you do, we can let you back into your account if you ever forget your password). However, you also won't be able to post new content or nominate criticisms without a verified email address.

You just go here and:

1. Pick a username

2. Pick a password

3. Type in some wavy letters

That's it. After you press "submit", your account will instantly be created and you'll be automatically logged into it. Then you can start voting on things.
