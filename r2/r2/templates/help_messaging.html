messaging

Messages are essentially an internal Sciteit email system. Messages can be sent from one user to another by clicking on the mail icon and following the prompts. Additionally, creators of categories have access to a seperate mailbox known as "modmail".

Sciteit will notify you of information, such as replies to links you have posted or new posts by your friends, by sending you a message through the messaging system. So keep an eye out for the orange mail icon near the top right of your screen!
